Requirements to join:

  If you have -

  • Received a campaign medal for overseas service;
  • Have served 30 consecutive or 60 non-consecutive days in Korea;
  • Have ever received hostile fire or imminent danger pay

  - then you're eligible to join the VFW

 

Joining the VFW is a great experience that you earned. Enjoy the comradery of fellow veterans, participate in community events, and get discounts for things like medical needs, insurance, travel services, and electronics such as cell phones and computers!
